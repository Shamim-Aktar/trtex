Handloom section

Handloom is a traditional craft which is practised by millions of people around the world. It's a skill passed down from generation to generation, with each person adding their personal touch to work. Handloom is an art that uses natural dyes and depends on them for their beauty and vibrancy. Handloom weaving first originated in ancient India. It was used as a form of resistance against foreign invaders and British colonists who sought to suppress native culture by banning everything from hand-spun cotton fabrics to silk saris woven by Indians.
